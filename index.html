Partner Practice Guides

Joined Hands Moving

Wednesday
Posture

Sit cross-legged facing each other. Masculine partner has palms face up, feminine places hands on top.

Action

As masculine breaths in, feminine breaths out; hands move down masculine's body. Move towards feminine and up as breaths switch. Continues in a synchronized circle.

Focus

Close eyes. Focus on physical synchronization—matching speed and turning points of breath.

Heart Breathing

Thursday
Posture

Sit facing. Right hand on partner's heart. Left hand over back of their hand (sandwiching hand against your heart).

Action

Breathe deeply into chest. Adjust rhythm until inhaling and exhaling in unison.

Focus

Visualize golden light growing in your heart on inhale, pouring through palm into partner on exhale.

Soul Gazing

Friday
Posture

Sit upright, knees touching or close.

Action

Soften gaze. Look into partner's left eye (or brow center). Minimize blinking, neutral face.

Focus

Look past personality. Silently ask, "Who is in there?" Witness consciousness looking back.

Circle of Light

Saturday
Posture

Sit in Yab Yum or cross-legged. Right hand (palm down) on partner's left (palm up). Same on other side.

Action

Inhale: draw energy (white light) from partner through left hand to heart. Exhale: send energy from heart down right arm to partner.

Focus

Create a literal "circuit" of energy flowing in a continuous loop between bodies.

Yab Yum Breathing

Sunday
Posture

One sits cross-legged; other straddles lap, legs around waist. Hug and touch foreheads.

Action

Synchronize breath completely. Inhale to expand against each other; exhale to melt into each other.

Focus

Focus on Third Eye connection. Dissolve sense of "two" into one breathing organism.

May this practice bring clarity, love, and truth.
